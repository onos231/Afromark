Welcome to SwapIt!

SwapIt is a simple and smart platform that helps people exchange food items directly with others in their community. Have extra rice and need yam? Post it, find a match, and swap with ease.

This app is made for everyone — whether you can read and write or prefer to speak, we’ve got you covered. Just tap, talk, and trade!

Save money, reduce waste, and help your neighbors — one food swap at a time.
