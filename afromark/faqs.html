Frequently Asked Questions
What is SwapIt? +
SwapIt is a food item swapping platform where users can exchange raw foodstuff with one another across Nigeria.
How do I post a swap? +
After registering and verifying your profile, go to the Post Swap page, add your item details, upload a picture or take one using your camera, then submit.
Is it free to use? +
Yes, SwapIt is free to use. However, users are responsible for their own delivery or collection logistics.
Do I have to meet the person I’m swapping with? +
You can decide whether to meet in person or use a delivery service. We recommend safe and public meeting places.
Can I report inappropriate behavior? +
Yes, go to the user’s profile and tap "Report User" if you encounter issues.
Is verification necessary? +
Verification builds trust. Verified users are more likely to have successful swaps.
Can I cancel my swap request? +
Yes, go to your My Swaps page, locate the request, and choose cancel.
How do I contact someone about their swap post? +
Tap on the post and use the messaging option to chat with the owner directly.
Can I swap more than one item at a time? +
Yes, you can post multiple items, and even bundle them in a swap if the other party agrees.
Do I need an internet connection to use SwapIt? +
Yes, a stable internet connection is required to view listings, chat, and post items.
