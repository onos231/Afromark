About Afromark

Our Mission

SwapIt is a platform designed to connect people who want to exchange raw food items fairly and efficiently across Nigeria. Our goal is to reduce food waste and promote community-based sharing.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s most trusted food exchange platform where everyone, regardless of literacy level, can access food without financial pressure.

What We’ve Achieved

Since launching, SwapIt has helped thousands of Nigerians swap food worth millions of Naira without spending money. We’ve built trust across regions and encouraged local trade and bonding.

Why SwapIt Works

By using technology that respects both the literate and non-literate population, SwapIt ensures inclusivity through voice commands, simple interfaces, and community trust scores.
