How it works

1. Signup

Create your free SwapIt account in seconds.

2. Post What You Have

Upload your extra food items and what you're looking to receive in return.

3. Browse Offers

Check what others have posted and find what food item you need near you.

4. Request a Swap

Send a swap request to another user. If they accept, you both arrange the exchange.

5. Swap and Save

Meet up and swap your food items. Everyone wins, saves cost and nothing goes to waste!
